## Getting Started

### 1. Create Your Account

1. Visit the BenwayLaw app
2. Click **"Register your firm"**
3. Fill in your details:
   - Your name and email
   - Create a password (8+ characters)
   - Your firm name (e.g., "Smith & Associates LLP")
   - Your province (this sets your tax rates)
4. Click **Create Account & Firm**

You'll be taken to your Dashboard with demo data already loaded!

---

## The Dashboard

Your Dashboard shows:

- **Active Matters** - How many open cases you have
- **Unbilled Time** - Work done but not yet invoiced
- **Outstanding A/R** - Unpaid invoices
- **Trust Balances** - Total funds held in trust

The **Risks & Opportunities** panel uses AI to highlight:
- Old unbilled time that should be invoiced
- Matters with no recent activity
- Trust account issues

---

## Managing Clients

### Add a New Client

1. Click **Clients** in the sidebar
2. Click **Add Client**
3. Enter their name, type (individual or organization), and contact info
4. Click **Create Client**

### View Client Details

Click any client name to see:
- Their contact information
- All their matters

---

## Managing Matters (Cases)

### Open a New Matter

1. Click **Matters** in the sidebar
2. Click **New Matter**
3. Select the client
4. Enter a matter name (e.g., "Contract Review - Software License")
5. Choose billing type (Hourly, Flat Fee, or Contingency)
6. Assign a responsible lawyer
7. Click **Create Matter**

A unique matter number (like 2025-001) is automatically generated!

### Matter Details

Each matter has tabs:

- **Overview** - Basic matter info
- **Time Entries** - All billable time
- **Invoices** - Related invoices
- **Trust Account** - Client funds held in trust
- **Activity** - Audit log of all actions

---

## Tracking Time

### Log Time on a Matter

1. Go to the matter's **Time Entries** tab
2. Click **Log Time**
3. Enter:
   - Date
   - Hours worked
   - Hourly rate
   - Description of work done
   - Check "Billable" if applicable
4. Click **Save Entry**

The amount is automatically calculated (hours √ó rate).

## Creating Invoices

### Generate an Invoice

1. Click **Invoices** ‚Üí **New** (or use the invoice generator)
2. Select a client with unbilled time
3. Check the time entries to include
4. Click **Generate Invoice**

The invoice automatically:
- Calculates subtotal from selected entries
- Applies your province's tax (HST/GST/PST)
- Creates a draft invoice

### Send & Record Payment

1. Open an invoice
2. Click **Mark as Sent** when you send it to the client
3. When paid, click **Record Payment** to log the amount

---

## Trust Accounting

### Important: Trust accounts hold client funds and have strict rules!

### Create a Trust Account

1. Go to a matter's **Trust Account** tab
2. Click **Create Trust Account**

### Record Deposits & Withdrawals

1. Click **New Transaction**
2. Choose Deposit or Withdrawal
3. Enter the amount, date, and description
4. Click **Confirm**

**Safety Rules:**
- You cannot withdraw more than the balance
- AI cannot execute trust transactions (only humans can)
- All transactions are logged in the audit trail

## Settings

Click **Settings** in the sidebar to:

- Update your firm name and province
- Manage team members and their roles
- Set default hourly rates
- Customize invoice number prefixes

### User Roles:

| Role | Can Do |
|------|--------|
| Admin | Everything - manage users, settings, all data |
| Lawyer | Manage clients, matters, invoices, time |
| Staff | View data, log time (limited permissions) |

---

## Tips for Success

1. **Log time daily** - Don't let unbilled work pile up
2. **Use the AI Command Bar** - It's faster than clicking around
3. **Check the Dashboard** - The risk panel catches things you might miss
4. **Reconcile trust monthly** - Keep client funds organized
5. **Invoice regularly** - The system will remind you about old unbilled time
